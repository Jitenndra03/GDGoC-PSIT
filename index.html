ABOUT

GDG Pranveer Singh Institute Of Technology is a dynamic, student-led community for developers, designers and tech enthusiasts who are passionate about Google's cutting-edge technologies. Our core mission is to create a vibrant ecosystem of learning and collaboration. We bring together passionate individuals to learn, share ideas and connect with like-minded peers and industry experts. Our events include hands-on workshops, insightful tech talks, collaborative study jams and exciting hackathons, covering a wide range of topics like Android, Web Development, Cloud Computing and AI/ML. By fostering knowledge sharing, mentorship and practical application of skills, we aim to empower the local developer community. Join us to grow your skills, build amazing projects and network with the innovators of tomorrow!
